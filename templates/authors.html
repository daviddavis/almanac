About the Authors

Ann VanderMeer

Ann VanderMeer is an American editor and publisher. She is the second female editor of Weird Tales magazine. During her tenure at Weird Tales, it won one Hugo award and three Hugo nominations.

The Time Traveler's Almanac is one of many anthologies she has edited with Jeff VanderMeer. Others have included The Weird, The New Weird, and three Steampunk anthologies. She has also founded Buzzcity Press and an online site dedicated to weird fiction called Weird Fiction Review.

Jeff VanderMeer

Jeff VanderMeer is an American author, editor, and publisher. Along with his wife, Ann, he has worked on several anthologies such as The Weird, The Thackery T. Lambshead Cabinet of Curiosities, and Best American Fantasy.

Jeff has also written several novels and short story collections such as his Ambergris series of books. He also released a new series in 2014 called Southern Reach. The first book, Annihilation, came out in February of 2014 with other to follow later in the year. Jeff also founded Weird Fiction Review.
